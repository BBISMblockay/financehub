How to read this table
Demand share — percent of filtered historical demand assigned to the family.
Scenario units — estimated units needed from the selected scenario.
Open PO — units already on open purchase orders.
Available + PO — current on-hand plus open PO units.
Net gap — scenario units minus available and on-order coverage.
Gap + buffer — net gap plus selected safety buffer.
Buy need — demand exceeds available / on-order coverage.
Review mix — mix, ASP, demand, or inventory relationship needs review.
Overstock — inventory coverage appears higher than scenario demand.
Covered — no immediate buy need based on the current scenario.
