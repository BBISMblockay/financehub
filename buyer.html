Read the Signals
Old inventory, too many weak choices, and stale categories can distort raw history. This model keeps 120-day category demand as the base anchor, but lets you rebalance toward comparable strength and growth/newness bias instead of blindly replaying the past.
Healthy use
Use historical weight for base demand truth, comparable weight for title fit, and growth uplift for intentional newness.
Bad use
Do not assume a weak stale category deserves the same level of buy just because it existed before.
